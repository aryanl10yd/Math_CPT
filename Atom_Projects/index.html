Business Problem

The question

The stock price follows a sinusoidal pattern: \( y = 10 \sin(\pi/6 t) + 50 \). What is the price after 6 months?

Solution: Substituting \( t = 6 \):

\( y = 10 \sin(\pi/6 \times 6) + 50 = 10 \sin(\pi) + 50 = 50 \).

Sports Problem

The question

A basketball is thrown, creating a triangle where the hoop is 10m high, and the launch angle is 45°. Solve for the distance of the throw.

Solution: Using trigonometry, the distance is \( d = 10 / \tan(45°) = 10 \).
