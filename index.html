Dear Reader,
As a student of the "Functions" course (MCR3U1), I wanted to share my thoughts on the successes and challenges students face in this program. This course has been an incredible opportunity to enhance mathematical skills, offering new perspectives, strengthening connections to previous material, and encouraging growth through correcting past mistakes. While rewarding, the journey has not been without its difficulties. The material started simply, but as new concepts and expressions were introduced, the complexity increased. Personally, I experienced setbacks, such as test stress and difficulty understanding certain topics. However, I also achieved significant successes, including participating in math contests from the University of Waterloo and applying trigonometry in robotics. Overall, this course has been a rewarding experience, preparing students for future academic challenges in Grade 12 and beyond.
Sincerely,
Aryan Gupta
Editor
